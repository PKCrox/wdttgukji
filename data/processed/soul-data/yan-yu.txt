## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 001–202
[역사] Yan Yu was younger brother to White Tiger Yan. In AD 197 Sun Ce attacked and Yan Yu was sent to parlay. They met face-to-face but when Sun Ce drew his blade Yan Yu jumped in fright, and was promptly cut down. Yan Yu had a reputation for bravery so, seeing this, his men became frightened and were quickly defeated. (1)

1) Sanguozhi: Wu 1
