## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 65–66
[역사] Zhou Cang, a fictional character, was described as a strong warrior with a dark face and a wiry beard. He originally took part in the Yellow Turban Rebellion, during which he met Guan Yu who impressed him due to his courage and honor. Though he wished to follow Guan Yu, Yu declined as he was transporting Liu Bei’s family. Zhou Chang became an independent renegade and moved on to Mt. Wo Niu with another former yellow turban rebel, Pei Yuanshao, where he earned a reputation for his strength and skill.
