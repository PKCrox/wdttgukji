## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 177–207
[역사] Yuan Shang was the youngest son of Yuan Shao. After Yuan Shao died, Yuan Shang managed to gain control, but gave a scant amount of troops to Yuan Tan. When Cao Cao besieged Yuan Tan later on, Yuan Shang arrived to assist. In AD 202, Yuan Shang dispatched Guo Yuan to seize the cities of Hedong commandery. He also attempted to ally with Ma Teng and others, but to no avail. Although Guo Yuan was initially successful, he ended up being defeated by Cao Cao’s general Zhong Yao at the Battle of Pingyang. In addition, Yuan Shang’s Xiongnu allies surrendered to Cao Cao. In the early months of the following year, Yuan Shang and Yuan Tan jointly were defeated at Liyang. This defeat came after nearly half a year of bitter fighting between the Yuan brothers and Cao Cao.
