## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 001–202
[역사] Known as White Tiger Yan, he was a local leader in the hills of Wu commandery, south of Qu’a. He formed a loose confederation that allied with Taschi Ci’s attempts to form a rival administration to Sun Ce’s. When Xu Gong was defeated by Sun Ce, he fled to Yan Baihu but only after Wang Lang surrendered did Sun Ce turn his attention to Yan Baihu.

Sun Ce defeated the confederation but Yan Baihu escaped to regroup his forces. Chen Yu worked secretly with Yan Baihu to destroy Sun Ce but the plan was defeated when Lü Fan attacked Chen Yu while Sun Ce led an expedition against Yan Baihu who escaped. Yan Baihu revolted again on Chen Deng’s encouragement, Sun Ce assassinated before he could launch a campaign against Yan Baihu.
[연의] Differences Between Fact and Common Fiction
	
		
	Yan Baihu was not killed by Dong Xi, he was still alive when Sun Ce was killed.
