## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 001–202
[역사] Cao Bao, formerly an officer of Tao Qian, served Liu Bei as Chancellor of Xiapi. In February of AD 196 he had a falling out with Zhang Fei and consequently Zhang Fei killed him (1), causing riots in the city. Xu Dan wrote to Lü Bu inviting him to attack Xiapi while also offering his support from within (2). Yuan Shu is also said to have written Lü Bu offering supplies (3).

(1): Accounts of this event differ. Zizhi tongjian describes the event as a ‘falling out’. Sanguozhi’s original text explains that Cao Bao rebelled and called for Lü Bu’s support. Hanmo yingxiong ji, quoted by Pei Songzhi in SGZ 7 explains that Zhang Fei intended to kill Cao Bao, but that Cao Bao took refuge in the encampment and called for Lü Bu.
