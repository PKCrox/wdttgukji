## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 001–202
[역사] Ma Tie was a son of Ma Teng. He accompanied his father to Ye city when Ma Teng was persuaded to take up rank there. Though granted a rank himself, he was executed alongside his father when Ma Chao later revolted.

A Biographical Dictionary of the Later-Han to Three Kingdoms by Dr. Rafe de Crespigny
