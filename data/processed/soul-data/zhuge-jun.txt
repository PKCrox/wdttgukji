## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 36–38
[역사] Zhuge Jun was the third son of Zhuge Gui and Zhuge Liang’s younger brother. After the death of Zhuge Gui, Zhuge Liang departed for Yuzhang and Zhuge Jun accompanied him. Zhuge Jun followed Zhuge Liang into service with Liu Bei and became a Colonel. (1)

1) Source: de Crespigny, Rafe, A Bibliographical Dictionary of the Later Han to the Three Kingdoms (AD 23–220).
