## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 28–29
[역사] Chen Zhen, styled Xiaoqi, hailed from Nanyang. When Liu Bei became Governor of Jing, Chen Zhen was hired to serve as a local officer and was stationed in various commanderies. When Liu Bei entered Shu, Chen Zhen remained in his service, where he progressed in rank and soon became Imperial Secretariat at the capital. When Sun Quan declared himself Emperor, Liu Bei sent Chen Zhen to offer his congratulations and to form an alliance, which he did, and they also fleshed out their countries’ border. When he died in AD 235, his son, Chen Ji, succeeded him.
