## Kongming 영문 백과사전
생몰: AD 63–65
[역사] Yan Yan hailed from Linjiang county in Ba commandery according to the Huayangguozhi. He served as the Grand Administrator of Ba under Liu Zhang. The Huayangguozhi claims that he served as General instead. Perhaps Yan Yan had both titles. When Liu Bei was invited into Shu by Liu Zhang and entered Ba commandery, Yan Yan clutched his heart and sighed over the invite, saying, “This can be said to be like sitting alone in a stranded mountain and arranging for a tiger to guard oneself!” Indeed, Liu Bei would revolt against Liu Zhang. Yan Yan was at Jiangzhou during the campaign, but he was defeated and captured alive by a general of Liu Bei, Zhang Fei.
